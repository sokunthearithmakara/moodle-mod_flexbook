# User Guide for Moodle FlexBook Mod

This documentation provides comprehensive guidance for students, teachers, and administrators using the Moodle FlexBook Mod. Each section contains detailed instructions pertinent to the user roles.

---

## For Students
### Navigating the View Page with Completion Tracking
1. **Accessing the View Page**: Navigate to the FlexBook module from your course page.
2. **Completion Tracking**: Check the completion status on your view page to see your progress against the set completion criteria.

### Distraction-Free Mode
- **Activating Distraction-Free Mode**: Click the 'Distraction-Free' button on the view page to enter a focused reading environment without distractions.

### Dark Mode
- **Enabling Dark Mode**: Toggle the Dark Mode button located at the bottom of the page for a more comfortable reading experience in low light.

### Experience Points
- **Earning Experience Points**: Participate in activities and complete modules to earn experience points that contribute to your overall progress.

---

## For Teachers
### Adding Activities
1. **Navigate to the Course**: Go to the course where you wish to add activities.
2. **Choose Activity Type**: Click on 'Add an Activity or Resource' and select the desired activity type.
3. **Configure Activity Settings**: Fill out the required fields and save changes.

### Editing Interactions
- **Modifying Existing Activities**: Click on the activity you wish to edit, adjust the settings, and save changes to apply updates.

### Accessing Reports
- **Viewing Reports**: Access reports by navigating to the course administration menu and selecting 'Reports' to analyze student progress and activity completion.

---

## For Administrators
### Site Settings
1. **Accessing Site Settings**: From the Site Administration panel, navigate to Site settings.
2. **Updating Settings**: Modify desired aspects of site functionality and appearance, then save changes.

### Content Type Management
- **Managing Content Types**: Add or remove content types by going to the Content Management section in the Site Administration panel.

### Appearance Settings
- **Configuring Appearance**: Adjust themes and layout settings under the Appearance settings in the Site Administration.

### Behavior Settings
- **Editing Behavior Settings**: Customize user behavior settings to optimize user experience under the Behavior settings menu.

### Report Field Configuration
- **Configuring Report Fields**: Tailor report fields to meet site needs by going to the report configuration section in Site Administration.

---

## Conclusion
This guide aims to equip users with the necessary tools and knowledge to make the most of the Moodle FlexBook Mod, facilitating a better learning and teaching environment.
